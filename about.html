About This Space

This site is a small, quiet corner of the web, created as a reminder of two paths that support liberation from suffering:

  • Vipassana meditation – the path of insight, stillness, and observation
  • Veganism – the path of compassion, non-harming, and respect for all beings

There is no agenda here — only gentle encouragement to pause, reflect, and perhaps make choices with greater care.
